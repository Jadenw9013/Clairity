# Clairity — Enhance AI Prompts

**Short Description**:
Automatically rewrite and restructure your prompts for better accuracy and results from ChatGPT, Claude, and more.

**Detailed Description**:
Clairity is an intelligent assistant designed to fix your prompts instantly before you send them to the AI.

Stop wasting time going back and forth with AI models because of vague, unstructured, or risky prompts. Clairity injects a small, convenient "Fix my question" button directly into your favorite AI interfaces (currently supporting ChatGPT and Claude).

**Key Features**:
* **Real-time Diagnostic Linting**: Instantly view tips to improve your prompt context and specificity.
* **Accuracy Risk Evaluation**: Understand if your prompt triggers sensitive domains or absolute constraints that frequently cause AI hallucinations.
* **Simple vs Advanced Modes**: Choose a single-click rewrite, or customize intent, tone, and formatting on-the-fly.
* **Privacy-First**: No prompts are stored or used for training.
