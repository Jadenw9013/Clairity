# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 2026

## 1. Data Collection & Usage
Clairity is designed with a privacy-first architecture. 
- **Prompts**: We do not store your prompts. When you click "Enhance," your prompt is temporarily sent to our backend exclusively for the duration of the request to communicate with the LLM. Once the rewrite is complete, the prompt is immediately discarded from memory.
- **Account Data**: We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII). Authentication relies strictly on anonymous Chrome Extension tokens.
- **Analytics**: Basic, anonymized telemetry (like error rates or rate limit triggers) is collected solely to maintain service health.

## 2. Third-Party Sharing
We send your prompt strictly to our designated AI providers (e.g., OpenAI or Anthropic) using their enterprise APIs. Their enterprise policies prohibit using data sent via API for model training.

## 3. Storage
User preferences, such as your chosen mode (Simple vs. Advanced), are stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`. This data never leaves your browser.

## 4. Contact
For any privacy inquiries or deletion requests, please contact us at privacy@clairity.app.
